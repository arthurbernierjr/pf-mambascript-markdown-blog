Who is Big Poppa Code?

Big Poppa Code is a seasoned technologist, educator, and mentor dedicated to empowering knowledge seekers through tech education and innovation. With years of experience in the tech industry and a deep commitment to breaking down barriers, he offers tailored mentorship, actionable insights, and strategic guidance through his platform, "Deeper Than Code." Big Poppa Code is not just a mentor but a catalyst for change, helping aspiring technologists and entrepreneurs navigate the complexities of the digital world, build successful careers, and make a lasting impact.

How Can Big Poppa Code Help Me?

Big Poppa Code can help you unlock your potential in the tech world by providing personalized mentorship, cutting-edge industry knowledge, and actionable strategies tailored to your goals. Whether you're looking to break into tech, start your own business, or scale your existing venture, Big Poppa Code offers the guidance and tools you need to succeed. Through his Deeper Than Code platform, you'll gain access to a wealth of resources, a supportive community, and the expertise needed to navigate and thrive in the ever-evolving digital landscape.

Big Poppa Code's Mission

- Empower the Black Community: Foster digital literacy and innovation within the Black community, equipping individuals with the skills and knowledge to succeed in tech and entrepreneurship.

- Bridge the Knowledge Gap: Provide accessible, in-depth education on emerging technologies, ensuring that everyone, regardless of background, can navigate and thrive in the digital economy.

- Create: Build tools, both digital + physical, that evangelize and empower our communities at scale

-Cultivate Future Leaders: Mentor and support the next generation of tech innovators and entrepreneurs, helping them to break barriers, build successful careers, and create lasting change.
